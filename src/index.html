What is Machine Intelligence and Why Leaf?

Machine Intelligence is concerned with algorithms through which machines can learn about their environment. These machines are able to solve complex and often narrow problems very efficiently.

Leaf is the platform with which these Machine Intelligence applications can be created. Leaf is a flexible, modular and portable Framework for numeric computations and can be used for many powerful Machine Intelligence paradigms, not only e.g. Deep Learning. We embrace Rust, a language which is very well suited for current state-of-the-art and future Machine Intelligence. It allows for the performance, memory-security, and extensibility, that other frameworks (TensorFlow, Caffe, Theano) only gain by combining high-level languages (Python, Lua) with low-level languages (C, C++).

Flexible + Performant

Leaf aims to be a flexible Machine Learning Framework while maintaining unachieved performance. Minimal dependencies and engineering Leaf from the ground up brings Leaf close to optimal efficiency. While you can flexible build complex machine learning models through composing layers. We designed Leaf and other projects to be as modular as possible, which means you can bring in new functionality through plugins others provide and are able to easily extend Leaf and the ecosystem.

Efficient + Scaleable

Leaf is truly portable and scalable. We designed Leaf, so that you can deploy your networks on various machines and platforms. Test your networks on your local desktop, train the same network on your servers and use the trained network on the smartphones of your users. Models expressed with Leaf efficiently use the available hardware resources on the machine. No matter if it is a tiny memory restricted device or a multi-GPU server, you just deploy your network and Leaf takes care of the rest.

Research + Production

Leaf is made for use in research and production. Researchers gain the advantage of an expressive, performant and easy-to-use Framework to develop new algorithms. Rust's package manager and Leaf's modular design lets you publish your work which makes it usable by other researchers or companies. Leaf tries to implement the latest research successes, bringing research and production closer together. No need to rewrite your network while going from testing to production.

We assist companies with know-how and hands on assistance in Machine Intelligence and Deep Learning. We work together with Data Science-, Engineering- and Business-teams to transfer knowledge, help integrating Machine Learning and building proof of concepts.
